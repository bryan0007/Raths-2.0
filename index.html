Rongai Boys Agri-Tech High School

Rongai Agricultural & Technical Secondary School was established in 1973 by the Christian Brothers. Rongai Agricultural & Technical Secondary is a secondary boarding school that offers both academic and agricultural skills to students aged between 12 and 18. The school maintains its own farm and produces all of its food. Students are taught how to grow cereals and vegetables and how to raise poultry , pigs and cows. They also learn farm management skills Rongai Agri-tech Boys High school currently serves 400 young men. Most students come from very poor amilies and are raised by single mothers. The biggest challenge students face in obtaining an education is affording school fees. Rongai has used previous twinning donations to cover school fees for less fortunate students This is one of the best performing schools in Nakuru County and produces many university students.
